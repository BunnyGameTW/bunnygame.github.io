📜Thoughts📜

I participated in FGJ with my friends, and this time, the questions were A and E with the highest number of votes from five patterns, and I could choose the two questions I wanted to play.

I think A looks like a magnified view from a telescope, and E looks like a pizza, and we all came up with our own ideas, but nothing was to everyone’s liking. Finally, the genre of narrative exploration is adopted: exploring life, gradually gaining the mask, and finally finding that the best ending is to take off the mask!

It was my first time to challenge this type of game in Game Jam, and although I didn’t finish it successfully, it gave me a new perspective on Game Jam: you don’t have to do what you are sure to finish, but what is challenging, even if the challenge is a long day of discussion and thinking, as long as you enjoy the fun. I hope to use this brainstorming experience to make the next event a smoother discussion!
